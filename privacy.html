Mindful Lock Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

Data Collection

Mindful Lock does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Local App Data

The app saves display settings locally on the user's device so the app and widget can show the selected passage and preferences. This data is not sent to the developer.

Third-Party Links

The app may open source reference links in the user's browser. Those external sites are outside the app and may have their own privacy practices.

Tracking And Analytics

The app does not use tracking, advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or third-party advertising.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please use the support page.
